About Us

Maqluba is a family-run Lebanese-Palestinian kitchen in Bay Ridge, NYC. We import our spices straight from Palestine and blend them with time-honored Lebanese recipes from Lebanon to bring you bold, authentic flavors.

As a small business, we pride ourselves on warm hospitality and a menu that celebrates our shared heritage.
